Horror Royale

In my spring semester of 2018, we were asked to create a project. The requirements were pretty minimal:

1. Must involve programming
2. Must be doable within 6 weeks.

We were able to create our final project within a group. So me and two of my friends decided to create a game in Unity. We wanted our game to be in a first person view and we wanted users to be fully immersed in the game. So we decided to make our game scary and hope to scare our friends with the game.
