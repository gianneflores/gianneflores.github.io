Paying People to Feed us and Getting New Hats

August 18, 2018

I finally got to hang out with Katie!!!! I have no idea when the last time we hung out was. Anyways we started our day eating brunch at Keke's. Katie has never been there and I haven't been there in a while so it was a good restaraunt to go to for the both of us. The food was pretty expensive though :/, but I guess that's every restaraunt. (WHY THINGS GOTTA COST $$$$$$$$ ?!?!?!?!?).? I ordered a banana pecan waffle, bacon, and potatoes wedges. The food was really gooodd and because there was a brunch of food (hehehe see what I did there) it was super filling. Super dank, very good! Would recommend. As a food enthusiast I give it a rating of 10/10.
